Introduction

How do we keep creative control in the age of AI while still using it to augment our skills, spend less time on the boring stuff and make work we're proud of?

Here are three elements of creative control to keep in mind when using AI.

Overview

The most important thing you can bring to your creative projects is an idea. AI is really bad at coming up with new ideas. It's where your human creativity will have the most impact. So don't just jump into the chat box,think about what you wanna make and why.

If your vision is a little fuzzy, you use AI as a sounding board for ideas, not a source for them. Research shows that when you start with an idea from AI, your creative thinking doesn't expand much further than that. The more decisions you stay in the driver's seat for, the more ownership you'll feel over the final product.

Slop

Slop is just what happens when you let AI take the wheel.

Writing a good prompt is one way to provide direction, but it doesn't stop there. Providing the context for your vision and prompts will get you even better outputs.

Help your AI assistant see the big picture, but have it start small.

If you're working with an agent, whether you're building in Lovable, workshopping a script with Claude, or using Descript’s own Underlord, try having it make a plan before executing anything or experiment with having the agent do smaller tasks with more direction.It's easier for you to review and iterate on, and it makes it less likely the agent will go rogue.

Breaking up your use of AI with review, iteration and good old fashioned manual work can help you stay engaged with the process. You can only look at an agent telling you its thinking for so long before your mind starts to wander away from your creation. Staying involved builds that sense of creative ownership.  That doesn't mean doing everything yourself.

It means being selective about what you offload to AI.AI can be a collaborator, production assistant or raw material. It can't be  creative for you. That's where you as a human creator shine.
